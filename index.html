About This Project

Why Sustainable Cooling?

Air conditioning accounts for a massive portion of global electricity consumption. Conventional systems rely on harmful refrigerants and energy-intensive compressors.

Greenflow Dynamics presents a breakthrough: a 2 kW sustainable air-conditioning system that replaces compressor-based cooling with Regenerative Indirect Evaporative Cooling (RIEC).

✓ Key Advantages

  • Uses only water as the cooling medium
  • Eliminates harmful refrigerants (HFCs/CFCs)
  • 75% less electrical power than conventional AC
  • Made from recyclable materials
  • Maintains comfortable humidity levels (56% avg)

How It Works

1

Air Intake

Hot ambient air enters through intake fans

2

Heat Exchange

Air passes through aluminum heat exchanger channels

3

Water Evaporation

Water is sprayed and evaporates, removing heat

4

Air Recirculation

Part of cooled air is recirculated for better efficiency

5

Supply Output

Cool, comfortable air delivered to occupants
